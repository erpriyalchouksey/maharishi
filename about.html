Welcome to The Maharishi Vidya Mandir School - Jabalpur

Maharishi Vidya Mandir (MVM) School, Jabalpur is a part of Maharishi Global Education Movement. In India Maharishi Vidya Mandir School chain is one of the largest school systems with 153 branches in 15 states. About 5717 teaching, administrative and support staff are busy training as many as 75670 students from KG to 12th standard.

  • The school is running under the Society MAHARISHI SHIKSHA SANSTHAN and the Registration is valid upto 99 years.
  • Maharishi Vidya Mandir, Jabalpur has been established in the year 1997 and is situated Maharishi Vidya Mandir - V area of Jabalpur in 2.0 acres of land.
  • Maharishi Vidya Mandir, Jabalpur like all other Maharishi Institutions is established to introduce Maharishiji's Consciousness Based Education System in mainstream school education to its students.

Maharishi Vidya Mandir, Jabalpur is affiliated to CBSE Delhi (Nursery to XIIth). The school has obtained NOC No. F-73-81/20-3/2008 on 30/06/2008 from the C.B.S.E. The school has a unique approach to complete and holistic education.
